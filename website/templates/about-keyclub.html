What is Key Club?

Key Club members around the world are learning how to lead and stand for what’s right through service and volunteerism . In partnership with their local Kiwanis club, high school students are making a positive impact as they serve others in their schools and communities .

"Key Club is an international, student-led organization that provides its members with opportunities to provide service, build character and develop leadership." - Key Club International Mission Statement

Key Club Hierarchy

Key club is an international , student-led organization, and thus, has a leadership hierarchy that connects individual clubs to the international leading body of students, ensuring that Key-Clubbers across the world can work towards unified goals and maintain strong values , no matter where they are. 

Club

 Starting at the club level, club cabinets are the frontline workers of Key Club, directly organizing and publicizing events in their local area for students in their high school. Club cabinets keep track of individual volunteering hours , hold general meetings and volunteering events, and, in essence, serve as the tip of the Key Club spear .

Division (D30 South)

Next, Division cabinets oversee operations across several individual clubs . Division 30 South is a great example. We manage 13 clubs in the local Anaheim/Cerritos vicinity. The DLT (division leadership team) holds monthly division volunteering and social events , called DCMS, helps organize inter-club events, and help lead groups of clubs within a common location by setting goals and recognizing members . Essentially, the division unifies and leads a local group of clubs.

District

 Ahh, the district, the vital bridge between Division and International. The district, as expected, leads a group of divisions. It sets goals, holds large, cross-division social, volunteering, and leadership events such as Fall Rally South and District convention

International

 At the international level, student-leadership sets the direction of Key Club on a long-term scale . This is where organization-wide goals are set, mission-statements and values are defined, and international leadership events are organized. Key Club is constantly growing , and the International cabinet is a big part of that.  

Key club Principles

Core Values

Leadership » Leadership is grounded in humility and service to others. A true leader listens, recognizes, and empowers.

Character building » Character is built by everyday acts of kindness, integrity and teamwork to grow stronger relationships and communities.

Caring » Compassion for others is a cornerstone of the Key Club experience, inspiring action and service to one’s community.

Inclusiveness » We welcome people of all backgrounds and ethnicities to join in serving and making a positive difference in our world.

Pledge

"I pledge, on my honor,
to uphold the Objects of Key Club International;
to build my home, school and community;
to serve my nation and world;
and combat all forces which tend to undermine these institutions."

Objectives of Key Club International

Key Club aims to cooperate with school principals and teachers to provide high school students with invaluable experience in living and working together and to prepare them for useful citizenship. Our members develop initiative and leadership skills by serving their schools and communities.

In doing so, we hope to promote and accept the following ideals :
  • To give primacy to the human and spiritual, rather than to the material values of life.
  • To encourage the daily living of the Golden Rule in all human relationships.
  • To promote the adoption and application of higher standards in scholarship, sportsmanship and social contacts.
  • To develop, by precept and example, a more intelligent, aggressive and serviceable citizenship.
  • To provide a practical means to form enduring friendships, to render unselfish service and to build better communities.
  • To cooperate in creating and maintaining that sound public opinion and high idealism which makes possible the increase of righteousness, justice, patriotism and good will.
