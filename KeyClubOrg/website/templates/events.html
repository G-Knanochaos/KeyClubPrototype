Title Bolsa Chica Trust
When 9:00AM - 12:00PM
What Volunteers will help plant and water native plants, remove non-native plants, pick up trash, and perform other maintenance tasks.
Why The Bolsa Chica Wetlands are one of the last standing wetland ecosystems in Southern California. They are crucial to the environment and all of its inhabitants. Volunteers can contribute to habitat conservation by volunteering with the Bolsa Chica Land Trust.
