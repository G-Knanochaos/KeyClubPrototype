About Our Division

Division 30 South was formed in the year 1996 when Angela Oh from Loara High School became our first Lt. Governor after the Division 30 split into North and South. Since the very beginning, our divisional mascot has always been the SUN where we shine while serving our communities. It has been 15 years since we became a division and we have continued to shine, grow, and learn, as we keep the power of the SUN alive.

We are proud and honored to have the 14 schools that make up Division 30 South: Anaheim, Cypress, Fairmont, Katella, Kennedy, Loara, Los Alamitos, Magnolia, Oxford, Rosary, Savanna, Servite, Western, and Whitney. Majority of our schools are centralized within the city of Anaheim, but we also have clubs within neighboring cities like Cypress, La Palma, Cerritos, and Los Alamitos. It is wonderful that our schools are located close together within a community filled with service events!
Our division is also sponsored by only 3 Kiwanis clubs: Cypress, La Palma, and Greater Anaheim. This creates a familiar environment for Kiwanians and Key Club officers to communicate effectively in order to help this division thrive. In the end, all 13 schools of Division 30 South have contributied so much to uphold a tradition of excellence for service and spirit.

Service, Unity, and Dedication. Division 30 South Suns truly know how to shine!
